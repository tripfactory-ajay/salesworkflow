KAM assigned where available; else Field Sales becomes SPOC. Single point for engagement, retention, escalations.
6
Quote Creation by Agent Agent
Agent creates quotes in Nexus platform. WhatsApp/Email enquiries converted to CRM entries.
7
Auto-Assignment System
System auto-routes quotes to destination support experts based on destination mapping. 24h SLA for manual assignment.

12

üîç Workflow Overlap Analysis & Recommendations

Strategic analysis of commonalities, divergences, and optimisation opportunities

üü¢ Major Overlaps (Centralise These)

  • Destination Expert Support: Both need destination specialists for complex itineraries
  • Quote Creation Logic: 3-option model, multi-destination handling, pricing rules
  • Operations Handover: Flight/hotel confirmations, voucher generation
  • Payments Processing: Deposit handling, balance follow-up, TAT tracking
  • Delivery Support: On-ground assistance, Holiday app, emergency handling
  • Post-Trip Reviews: Feedback collection, Google reviews, corrective actions
  • SLA Management: 24h escalation rules, quality tracking
  • Training Content: Destination knowledge, product updates, visa rules

üî¥ Key Divergences (Keep Separate)

  • Acquisition Model: Direct leads vs Agent self-registration
  • Qualification: FCD (B2C) vs Verification Team (B2B)
  • Sales Approach: Direct selling vs Enabling agent to sell
  • Relationship Type: Transactional (per trip) vs Partnership (ongoing)
  • Training Focus: Sales skills (B2C) vs Platform + Marketing tools (B2B)
  • Account Management: Individual experts vs SPOC/KAM model
  • Quote Origin: Expert-created vs Agent-created
  • Growth Strategy: Referrals (B2C) vs PCP/Partner programmes (B2B)

üü° Optimisation Opportunities

  • Shared Expert Pool: Train experts on both B2C consultative selling AND B2B agent support
  • Unified CRM: Single customer view - track both direct customers AND agents
  • Smart Routing: Same auto-assignment engine for both streams
  • Common Content Library: Flyers, itineraries, destination guides for both
  • Cross-Training: B2C experts can handle B2B overflow and vice versa
  • Shared Services: One payments team, one ops team, one delivery team
  • Feedback Integration: Agent feedback (B2B) + Customer feedback (B2C) ‚Üí Product improvements
  • Hybrid Model: High-value B2C leads could become "agent-like" with dedicated SPOC
